About Retro Classic Car

Established for a decade we are based in scenic North Yorkshire within easy
access of the A19 and A1 road networks.

The classic cars we look for and sell are always ones that we are interested in getting behind the wheel and driving for ourselves. These being primarily iconic sports and prestige classic cars from the 60’s, 70’s and sometimes from more recent times as certain marques and models take on a more classic appeal and standing such as Ferrari and Aston Martin. But our speciality is what we like best - classic American coupes and convertibles with great names such as Mustang, Corvette, Firebird and Camaro. You will also find a sprinkling of some of the best that Britain has to offer in marques such as Triumph, MG, Jaguar and TVR.

All our cars have the same thing in common in that apart from being an investment they are wonderful just to look at but also offer a unique and fun driving experience. Have a look at our current cars for sale and some of those we have sold in the last 10 years.
